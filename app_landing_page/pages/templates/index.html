Checkit

A location sharing app that lets you see where your friends are before you get there.

Easy

Simply open Checkit to see which friends are nearby or drop a pin so others can see you.

Private

Select only the specific friends you want to see your location for each event.

Disappearing

Your location disappears when the event is over or when you leave.
